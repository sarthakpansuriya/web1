10 Veg Superfoods for a Healthier Diet

1. Kale

Kale is a nutrient-dense leafy green packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

2. Spinach

Spinach is a great source of iron, calcium, and magnesium, making it excellent for bone health.

3. Broccoli

Broccoli is high in fiber, vitamins C and K, and known for its cancer-fighting properties.

4. Carrots

Carrots are rich in beta-carotene, which supports eye health and boosts the immune system.

5. Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are packed with fiber and vitamins, making them a fantastic addition to a healthy diet.

6. Brussels Sprouts

Brussels sprouts are loaded with antioxidants and help reduce inflammation.

7. Avocado

Avocados are full of healthy fats, fiber, and essential vitamins like E and K.

8. Cauliflower

Cauliflower is low in calories but high in vitamins C, K, and folate, great for heart health.

9. Beets

Beets are rich in nitrates, which help improve blood pressure and boost exercise performance.

10. Peas

Peas are a great source of plant-based protein and rich in fiber and micronutrients.
