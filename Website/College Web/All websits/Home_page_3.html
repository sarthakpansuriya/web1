Hydration Tips for Optimal Health

1. Drink Water Regularly

Water is the primary source of hydration. Make sure to drink at least 8 glasses of water a day.

2. Eat Hydrating Foods

Fruits like watermelon, cucumbers, and oranges are great sources of water and nutrients.

3. Limit Caffeinated Drinks

Too much caffeine can dehydrate you. Drink more water instead of coffee or soda.

4. Stay Hydrated During Exercise

Always bring water when exercising. Dehydration can affect performance and recovery.
